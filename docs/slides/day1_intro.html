Bioconductor is an organization

Advisory Boards help shape the direction of the project and ensure technological relevance:

  • Bioconductor Foundation / European Bioconductor Society
    501(c)(3) organization, handles money regarding conference organization, donations, and sponsorship

  • Scientific Advisory Board (SAB)
    Provides external guidance and oversight of the scientific direction of the project. Invitation-only board.

  • Technical Advisory Board (TAB)
    Advises on project and package-level infrastructure. Open call; elections held annually.

  • Community Advisory Board (CAB)
    Dedicated to developing and diversifying the Bioconductor community. Open call; elections held annually.

  • Core Team
    Developers maintaining core packages and infrastructure.

  • Global Bioconductor Community üéâ
